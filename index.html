Wizard with a Sword

For my senior project at CSULB, over a year, my team developed an independent video game called Wizard with a Sword in the Unity Game Engine. Wizard with a Sword was a top-down rogue-lite that contained two areas filled with many different types of enemies and ended with an Epic boss battle. Our game had state machine based enemy ai, procedurally generation levels, physics based combat and much more.

ChessAI

I developed a Chess AI using the machine learning library TensorFlow. It was built in python and I used training data composed of chess board positions with evaluations from StockFish. The chess AI was able to hold its own against the world's strongest chess engine for quite a while.
